Who We Are

Founded in 2005, Luxury Travel has grown from a small boutique agency to one of the most respected names in luxury travel. Our team of expert travel designers combines local knowledge with global expertise to craft journeys that go beyond the ordinary.

We believe that travel should be transformative, not just transactional. That's why we go above and beyond to create experiences that will stay with you long after you return home.
